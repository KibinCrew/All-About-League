WHAT IS LEAGUE OF LEGENDS?
This is a multiplayer online battle arena that was first released in the year 2009. A game, in which two teams of five players will try to destroy the enemy's Nexus. Players control unique champions with different abilities and choose to play in one of three lanes: Top, Mid, or Bot, and one player roams the Jungle. The teams need to destroy towers, secure their objectives, and dominate the map. With deep strategic gameplay mixed with constant updates, this game remains a favorite for both casual and professional e-sport arenas around the world.
LARGE CHAMPION POOL
League of Legends has a total vast champion pool of over 160 unique champions to choose from, which means the game will always feel different and strategic with every play. Because different champions have different abilities, play styles, and are capable of filling different roles, the game really is very fun to play. Players can opt for damage dealers, protectors, or even champions that can control the battlefield to carry out their preferred style of gameplay. All this variety offers an enormous number of possible team compositions and strategies to keep things dynamic and challenging.
